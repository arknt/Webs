Awwwards: Orken World

Visit Website

The website Orken World is an intriguing website that embodies the medieval and fantastic style. It certainly achieves its purpose of conveying the fantastic aesthic through various interactive elements, animations and a well established identity. The color palette focused on warm colors such as orange works very well for making the page recognizable and also to stand out. There is also a well balanced and cautious choice for images, illustrations and typographies, managing to convey both futurism and fantasy.

CSS Design Awards: Modden

Visit Website

Out of all selected websites, Modden has to be one of the most unique ones. We can appreciate interactive functions that mainly consist on animations whenever the mouse is on top of an element of the website. The most characteristic animation is that whenever you have your mouse on the look button, in this scenario the whole website falls. As mentioned before, it has several functionalities that really make this site come to life and feel like a real experience to the user. Additionally, it can also serve as fun way to keep them engaged with discovering everything they can do.
