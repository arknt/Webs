About my Digital Album

This project is a digital album that showcases images from my childhood, each one telling a unique story. This digital album has the purpose of serving me and my family as a way to preserve the past and treasure the moments captured in each photo. The main theme of the project is memory and how it is very common for us to forget various moments in our lives. As you can appreciate I did not make a very good job remembering most of what was going on in each photo, nonetheless my digital albums at representing how cloudy our memories can be, this is also the reason the images are in disorder.
